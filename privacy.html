Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

FitMind AI (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the PUSH mobile application (“the App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account data: Name, email address (via email/password or Apple Sign-In).
  • Profile data: Age, gender, height, weight, fitness level, goals, dietary preferences, and food allergies.
  • Workout data: Exercise logs, completed workouts, personal records.
  • Nutrition data: Meal logs, calorie and macro tracking.

Information collected automatically

  • Health data: With your permission, we read data from Apple HealthKit (steps, heart rate, active energy, sleep). We do not write to HealthKit.
  • Usage data: App interactions, feature usage, and crash reports (via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics).
  • Device data: Device type, OS version, and app version.

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or camera.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • OpenAI: AI-generated workout and nutrition plans. Your profile data is sent to generate plans but is not stored by OpenAI for training.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management.
  • Apple HealthKit: Health data is read locally and is never sent to our servers or shared with third parties.

4. HealthKit Data

PUSH integrates with Apple HealthKit to enhance your experience. Important:

  • HealthKit data is only accessed with your explicit permission.
  • HealthKit data is processed locally on your device.
  • We do not store HealthKit data on our servers.
  • We do not share HealthKit data with any third party.
  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising or marketing.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely in Firebase Cloud Firestore, hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure.
  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL.
  • We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your data.
  • We retain your data only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the App.
  • Correct inaccurate data through your profile settings.
  • Delete your account and all associated data (available in Settings > Delete Account).
  • Withdraw consent for HealthKit access at any time through iOS Settings.
  • Export your data by contacting us.

7. Data Retention

When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

PUSH is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 16, we will delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own (e.g., United States for Firebase and OpenAI). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
